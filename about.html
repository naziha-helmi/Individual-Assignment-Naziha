ABOUT

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is Malaysia's largest university, offering modern education with advanced facilities across 34 campuses, 4 colleges, 14 faculties, and academic centers nationwide. UiTM provides over 500 programs, including Foundation, Pre-Diploma, Diploma, Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD levels, along with Professional Programs. The university aims to make higher education accessible, contributing to the country's development by unlocking potential and shaping the future.

Recently, UiTM achieved notable rankings, including 587th in the QS World University Rankings 2025, 101st in the QS Asia University Rankings 2025, and 607th in the QS World University Rankings: Sustainability 2025. It is ranked 151st in Asia and 11th in Malaysia. UiTM’s Hospitality and Leisure Management program is ranked among the top 50–100 globally, and 18 subjects are listed in the QS World University Rankings by Subject 2024. Additionally, UiTM placed 136th in the Times Higher Education (THE) Interdisciplinary Science Rankings 2025 and won the 3G SDGs Championship Award in 2022.

UiTM has also been recognized with other prestigious awards. In June 2022, it won the Reader’s Digest Trusted Brand Gold Award for the 12th time in the public university category. It also received The BrandLaureate BestBrands Awards for the first time. For the UI GreenMetric World University Rankings 2024, UiTM ranked 107th globally and 6th in Malaysia. With over 770,000 alumni in fields like science, technology, humanities, and entrepreneurship, UiTM creates opportunities to develop leaders at national, industry, and global levels. The university is on track to become a globally recognized institution by 2025.
